100% safe and unruggable

We value the safety of our investors' funds above everything else. That's why we took the steps to ensure FullWallet is 100% safe and technically unruggable. The initial liquidity is locked away forever. For the auto-added liquidity by our smart contract, we burn the LP Tokens (think "ticket to liquidity pool") regularly so that it's technically impossible to remove liquidity at any time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our team are more than happy to answer any questions you may have, most of our discussion takes places in our Telegram Channel if you want to ask us anything personally. Otherwise you can find some frequentyly asked questions below.

  • How does the transaction tax work?

    FW has a 2% transaction tax. check our tokenomics section

  • When will you burn more tokens?

    very low supply currently, our Black Hole burns on each transaction. And that burn is continuously accelerating!

  • What makes FW different from other coins?

    We think our strong points are our transparency, strong work ethic and commitment to push our boundaries as much and as often as possible. And of course, as a FW holder, we basically pay you to hold the token.

  • How can I make sure I don't get scammed by clone projects

    Always use ONLY our official Telegram channel. Our team will NEVER DM you to ask about anything. Be EXTREMELY suspicious about anyone DM-ing you with an offer to help, airdrop or generally asking you personal information such as your wallet seed phrase. NEVER give out such information, block and report those persons immediately.

  • When trading on Pancakeswap, do I use V1 or V2?

    Use V1, that is the original Pancakeswap liquidity pool. Since our liquidity is locked forever, we cannot move it to V2. It is technically possible to trade in V2, but that pool will contain liquidity added by users so there might be a price difference between pools.

  • When trading CEXes (WhiteBit, BitMart, Gate.io) do transaction taxes apply?

    No, transaction taxes do not apply on CEXes, they don't support it.

  • When sending FW to CEXes (WhiteBit, BitMart, Gate.io) do transaction taxes apply?

    Yes, any transfer incurs the 4% tax. This also applies when you send funds to a CEX or any other wallet.
